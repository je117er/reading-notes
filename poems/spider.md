### Spider
*by Andrea Gibson*

All of my pain is a spider  
I've learned not to crush   
with the heel of my shoe    

but to guide with a page    
of my journal   
into an empty glass 

I ask questions about   
its life, its purpose   
as I walk careful   

out to the garden and rest  
it down on the earth.   
My pain--how happy  
it is to leave me   
whenever I treat it     
kind.
