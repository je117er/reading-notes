### Interpretations
*by Mourid Barghouti*

A poet sits in a coffee shop, writing.  
The old lady    
thinks he is writing a letter to his mother.    
the young woman     
thinks he is writing a letter to his girlfriend.    
the child   
thinks he is drawing.   
the businessman 
thinks he is considering a deal.    
the tourist     
thinks he is writing a postcard.    
the employee    
thinks he is calculating his debts.     
The secret policeman    
walks, slowly, towards him.


### People Like Us
*by Robert Bly*

There are more like us. All over the world  
There are confused people, who can't remember   
The name of their dog when they wake up, and    
&emsp; people  
Who love God but can't remember where

He was when they went to sleep. It's    
All right. The world cleanses itself this way.  
A wrong number occurs to you in the middle      
Of the night, you dial it, it rings just in time    

To save the house. And the second-story man     
Get the wrong address, where the insomniac lives,   
And he's lonely, and they talk, and the thief   
Goes back to college. Even in graduate school,  

You can wander into the wrong classroom,    
And hear great poems lovingly spoken    
By the wrong professor. And you find your soul  
And greatness has a defender, and even in death     
&emsp; you're safe
